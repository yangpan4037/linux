官网找到最新版本安装包
http://tomcat.apache.org/download-80.cgi

找到想要安装的版本，拷贝地址
http://mirror.bit.edu.cn/apache/tomcat/tomcat-8/v8.5.20/bin/apache-tomcat-8.5.20.tar.gz


下载到/usr/local/src
cd /usr/local/src
wget http://mirror.bit.edu.cn/apache/tomcat/tomcat-8/v8.5.20/bin/apache-tomcat-8.5.20.tar.gz


解压到/usr/local
tar -zxvf /usr/local/src/apache-tomcat-8.5.20.tar.gz -C /usr/local/
