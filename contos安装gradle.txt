官网找到最新版的安装包：
https://services.gradle.org/distributions/

找到想要安装的版本，拷贝地址
https://services.gradle.org/distributions/gradle-4.1-bin.zip


下载到/usr/local/src
cd /usr/local/src
wget https://services.gradle.org/distributions/gradle-4.1-bin.zip


解压到/usr/local
unzip -o -d /usr/local/ /usr/local/src/gradle-4.1-bin.zip


配置环境变量
vi /etc/profile
# gradle settings start 2017-08-23 by yangpan
GRADLE_HOME=/usr/local/gradle-4.1
export PATH=${GRADLE_HOME}/bin:${PATH}

立即生效
source /etc/profile

验证是否安装成功
gradle -version
